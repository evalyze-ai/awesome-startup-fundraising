## **Pitch Deck Review & Scoring**

Tools that help you build, structure, or analyse pitch decks.

- [**Evalyze.ai**](https://evalyze.ai/?utm_source=github&utm_medium=awesome_list&utm_campaign=startup_fundraising_resources) → AI-assisted deck and startup evaluation with investor-readiness scores, risk flags, and concrete suggestions before outreach.

- [**Slidebean**](https://slidebean.com/) → Pitch deck design and analytics platform with templates and investor-focused guidance.

- [**Beautiful.ai**](http://Beautiful.ai) → Presentation builder with smart templates that keep pitch decks visually consistent.

- [**Tome**](https://landing-staging.tome.app/) → AI-native storytelling tool that turns outlines and product docs into pitch-style slide narratives.

- [**Gamma**](https://gamma.app/) → Browser-based deck and doc tool that turns prompts or text into interactive, deck-like pages.

- [**Canva Presentations**](https://www.canva.com/presentations/) → General-purpose design tool with pitch deck templates and real-time collaboration.

## **Investor Discovery & Matching**

Platforms for finding, qualifying, and prioritising investors.

- [**OpenVC**](https://www.openvc.app/) → Founder-friendly investor directory with filters for stage, geography, and thesis, plus open submission format.

- [**Signal (NFX)**](https://signal.nfx.com/) → Free tool for mapping warm intros and relationships to investors across your extended network.

- [**Crunchbase**](https://www.crunchbase.com/) → Database of companies, investors, and funding rounds for market and investor research.

- [**PitchBook**](https://pitchbook.com/) → Private-markets data platform covering VC, PE, and M\&A transactions.

- [**Evalyze.ai**](https://evalyze.ai/?utm_source=github&utm_medium=awesome_list&utm_campaign=startup_fundraising_resources) → Investor discovery and matching based on round, sector, geography, and thesis, tied to your deck score.

- [**Dealroom**](https://dealroom.net/) → Startup and VC database, widely used for ecosystem mapping (especially strong in Europe).

## **CRM & Dealflow Management**

CRMs and pipeline tools are often repurposed for tracking a fundraiser.

- [**Affinity**](https://www.affinity.co/product/crm) → Relationship-intelligence CRM that auto-captures emails/meetings and helps track dealflow across the firm.

- [**Attio**](https://attio.com/) → Flexible, AI-native CRM used by modern GTM and investing teams to model pipelines and workflows.

- [**Streak**](https://www.streak.com/) → Gmail-native CRM; manage fundraising pipelines directly from your inbox.

- [**HubSpot CRM**](https://www.hubspot.com/products/crm) → General-purpose CRM with contact, company, and pipeline tracking, popular with early-stage teams.

- [**Pipedrive**](https://www.pipedrive.com/) → Deal-centric CRM with visual pipelines that many founders reuse for investor outreach.

- [**Close**](https://www.close.com/) → Sales engagement CRM with built-in calling, email, and sequences some founders use for investor outbound.

## **Data Rooms & Document Management**

Places to host decks, KPIs, and due diligence documents.

- [**DocSend**](https://www.docsend.com/) → Virtual data room and deck-sharing tool with page-level analytics for fundraising and investor updates.

- [**Papermark**](https://www.papermark.com/) → Open-source virtual data room and secure document sharing with analytics and custom domains/branding.

- [**Google Drive**](https://workspace.google.com/products/drive/) → General cloud storage and sharing; common “good enough” data room for early rounds.

- [**Notion**](https://www.notion.com/) → All-in-one workspace for docs and databases; often used to host lightweight data rooms and FAQs.

- [**Dropbox**](https://www.dropbox.com/) → A File sync and sharing platform frequently used for larger diligence folders.

---

## **Email Outreach & Sequencing**

Cold email and sequence tools for investor outreach (and sales).

- [**Instantly**](https://instantly.ai/) → Cold email platform focused on deliverability, multiple inboxes, and campaign analytics.

- [**Lemlist**](https://lemlist.com/) → Multichannel outreach (email \+ LinkedIn \+ phone) with strong personalisation features.

- [**Mailshake**](https://mailshake.com/) → Sales engagement and cold email tool combining email, phone, and social touches.

- [**Apollo**](https://www.apollo.io/) → Sales intelligence plus multichannel sequences; often used for highly targeted investor lists.

## **Founder Education & Knowledge Bases**

Long-form content that teaches you how to fundraise and build.

- [**YC Startup Library**](https://www.ycombinator.com/library) → Essays, videos, and podcasts on starting up, including seed fundraising basics.

- [**Startup School (YC)**](https://www.startupschool.org/) → Free online course on how to start a company and raise a first round.

- [**First Round Review**](https://review.firstround.com/) → Tactical, long-form articles on product, hiring, leadership, and fundraising.

- [**a16z Articles & Podcasts**](https://a16z.com/podcasts/a16z-podcast/) → Venture and tech content from Andreessen Horowitz partners and operators.

- [**Techstars Blog & Education**](https://www.techstars.com/education) → Playbooks, talks, and courses from the Techstars accelerator network.

## **VC / Angel Networks & Platforms**

Platforms where investors and founders actually meet.

- [**AngelList**](https://www.angellist.com/) → Platform for startups, angel investors, and funds (syndicates, rolling funds, talent).

- [**Seedrs**](https://thecrowdspace.com/platform/seedrs/) → Equity crowdfunding platform enabling individuals to invest in European startups.

- [**Republic**](https://republic.com/) → Investment platform opening private-market deals (including startups) to a broader investor base.

- [**Crowdcube**](https://www.crowdcube.com/) → Equity crowdfunding platform where individuals invest in startups and growth companies.

- [**Gust**](https://gust.com/) → Platform with tools for startups and investor groups, from company setup to dealflow management.
